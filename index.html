The Origin Story

March 15

Two Kevins, Jossendal and McGahey, who were top chefs came together to open a steakhouse like nobody had seen before. Their unique styles created flavors nobody can recreate. Since 1981 Kevins has been open in the heart of Las Vegas serving the finest delicacies around.
